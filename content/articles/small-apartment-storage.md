## Design upward before spreading outward

Small spaces reward vertical thinking. Tall shelving, wall-mounted lighting, and slim storage pieces free the floor while making the room feel taller and more composed.

![Small apartment with layered storage](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1522708323590-d24dbb6b0267?auto=format&fit=crop&w=1400&q=85)

## Choose furniture that works twice

The most useful small-space pieces solve more than one problem. A bench can hold throws, a console can become a desk, and a wall shelf can replace a bulky bookcase.

## Let one zone breathe

Even a compact apartment needs visual rest. Keep one wall or corner quieter than the rest so the home does not feel like every inch is shouting for utility.
