## Let the bed look lived in

The best linen bedrooms do not feel overly pressed. Mix a fitted sheet, relaxed duvet, and two layers of pillows in close tones so the bed feels soft but considered.

![Neutral linen bedroom](https://images.unsplash.com/photo-1618221118493-9cfa1a1c00da?auto=format&fit=crop&w=1400&q=85)

## Balance both sides without matching everything

Use similar heights on each side of the bed, but vary the objects. One side can hold a stone table lamp while the other uses a sculptural vase and a stack of books.

## Make lighting warmer than you think

Bedroom lighting should flatter materials and people. Use warm bulbs, shaded lamps, and indirect light so the room transitions gently from day to night.
